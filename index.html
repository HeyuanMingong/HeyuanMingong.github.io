About me

I am now an Associate Research Fellow in Nanjing University. My research interests include reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms and their applications in robotics, operations research, and financial technology.
Specifically, I work on how learning algorithms can scale RL agents to dynamic environments (or "open" environments), allowing them to autonomously adapt to the non-stationary task distributions in real-world domains. This includes a wide range of topics such as incremental learning, lifelong learning, transfer learning, and meta-learning.
